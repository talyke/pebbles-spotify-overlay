Authenticate with Spotify

You need to log in with your Spotify account to use this application. This will give PebblesSpotifyOverlay permission to access your podcast library and control playback.

Benefits of connecting:

  • Access your podcast library
  • Get personalized podcast recommendations
  • Control playback with advanced features
  • Auto-skip ads during podcasts
  • Customize your podcast viewing experience
